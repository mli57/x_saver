# X Saver
A lightweight Chrome extension for saving and organizing posts from X (formerly Twitter),
offering a cleaner alternative to X's native bookmarks.

## Features
- **Save posts** directly from your feed via a "Save" button at the bottom of each post
- **Search** saved posts by title or content
- **Filter** by author (alphabetical), time posted, or recently saved
- **Post counter** displaying your total number of saved posts
- **Clear all** button to reset your saved posts back to 0
